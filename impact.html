Our Purpose

Our mission and purpose is to accelerate the ecomonic opportunities of Irelands greenways as a sustainable and socially ethical tourism opportunity.

Why?

We think that most greenway users would like Irelands greenways to benefit the local communities. In a world where the larger travel companies often dominate our travel business, Irelands greenways provide an opportunity for small niche businesses to provide exceptional services directly to customers.


We believe that by building a marketplace for passionate local providers on which they can share environmentally sustainable and ethically run tourism services, we can complete our mission.

Join us

Ireland greenways is an online community marketplace for people to list, discover, and book tourism products along the greenways. The marketplace allows providers to get exposure by listing their services, rooms, eateries, hire services and tours. They will be able to take bookings seamlessly and build up a reputation online.


You can support our mission by booking your next adventure through Irelands Greenways.
